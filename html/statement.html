Artist Statement

This is a response. A response to people, places, and things. This is constantly put in front of my face, appearing and disappearing on a whim. It comes into plain view every time I ride the subway, get a coffee, turn on the television. Recently, after a less than unpleasant encounter, I decided I would make this exactly as I know it to be. I have seen it many times, and in many different places, but I have never seen it as a tangible object of my reality. So this is what I have produced. I am sure there is a story behind this, rather than a sentence, or a snappy title. Whether it be unresolved, or non-readable, this truly is an accurate replica of my fantastical response to some people, some places, and some things. 2007
