On June 28th, 2014, I put on my first annual student and friend Musical Spectacular at the Waltham Public Library. The recital featured 10 of my students and 10 of my friends, performing everything from classic piano compositions to vocal improvisations with ukelele, guitar, you name it! It was spectacular to say the very least. Below are some videos taken of some of the performances. More to come... Enjoy!
